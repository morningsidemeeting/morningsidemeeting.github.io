[The People's Campaign for Parole Justice](https://www.parolejusticeny.com/) will hold an all-day virtual advocacy day on Tuesday, January 11, 2022. Participants will have the opportunity to meet with legislators and join a press conference in support of parole justice and the passage of the [Elder Parole](https://www.nysenate.gov/legislation/bills/2021/S15) and [Fair & Timely Parole](https://www.nysenate.gov/legislation/bills/2021/S1415) bills. [RSVP here](bit.ly/ParoleJusticeJan11).

[Release Aging People in Prison (RAPP)](https://rappcampaign.com/), with which many Friends are associated, is a campaign partner for this event.

_[A Look at the New York Fair & Timely, Elder Parole Bills](https://interrogatingjustice.org/supervised-release/new-york-fair-timely-parole/)_, Interrogating Justice, March 30, 2021
