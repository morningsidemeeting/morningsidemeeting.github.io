**Meeting for Worship**

Morningside Meeting will hold our next **meeting for worship on March 6 at 11 a.m.: a hybrid in-person/remote meeting for worship,** followed by afterwords, announcements, and introductions of visitors and those new to Morningside. The virtual meeting room will be open at 10:45 a.m. for Friends and attenders to begin to gather. If you are new to Morningside, or not on the email list, and wish to attend, please email: morningsidequakermeeting@gmail.com.

Meeting With Children is back every First Day (Sunday)! If you have questions about bringing your child with you to worship, please email morningsidequakermeeting@gmail.

**For those who wish to participate at Morningside worship in person**, please note the following:

- In-person Meeting for Worship will occur at Riverside Church **on the 12th Floor.**
- At this time, masks are required for in-person worship.

Morningside Meeting Ministry and Counsel is a committee charged with the pastoral care of the meeting. Members are available to answer questions about Quakerism, about Friends Meeting, and to provide support. To reach the committee, please email: morningsidequakermeeting@gmail.com.

The full text of _Faith and Practice, the Book of Discipline of the New York Yearly Meeting of the Religious Society of Friends_ can be found at https://nyym.org/faith-and-practice.
